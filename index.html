Welcome to the work of onesilveredvein - jewellery design by Vivienne McGregor

Profile

onesilveredvein is a collection of original, one-off jewellery desins by Vivienne McGregor.

Inspired by contemporary art, sculpture, architecture, she creates hand-crafted free-form experiments in fine silver and Sterling silver.

Working in both silver Precious Metal Clay (PMC) and Sterling silver, each item is custom-made, bespoke, ensuring the wearer a unique experience.
